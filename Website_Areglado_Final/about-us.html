Directions

Travelling by car

-22 kilometers from the port of Samal to Muni-Muni Resort (30-45 minutes ride)

  • Take the barge from Sasa Ferry Wharf to Samal Island (15-20 minutes ride from the port)
  • After getting off the barge, make a LEFT TURN
  • Upon approaching the roundabout (rotonda), make a RIGHT TURN
  • Proceed to Pe√±aplata, after passing the school make a RIGHT TURN (follow the sign going to the wet market)
  • Follow the sign to get back to the main road going to Canibad
  • When you reach uphill, there is a fork road. Take the LEFT ROAD
  • After Aundanao Elementary School, make a RIGHT TURN
  • After you pass Canibad and the free flowing spring water on the first uphill.
  • Muni-Muni is on your left side.
  • Drop off all your stuff and passengers. Parking is on the upper level.

Travelling by HABAL-HABAL, TRICYCLES, or BAO BAO'S

-available at the port of Samal Island.

Travelling by BOAT

-Boats are chartered when going to Muni-Muni Resort.

  • Guest must make an advance arrangement with the boat operators at Sta Ana Wharf.
  • Travel time is approximately 2 hours and 30 minutes.
